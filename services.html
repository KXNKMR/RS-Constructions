Services

Waterproofing

RS Constructions provides reliable waterproofing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Our waterproofing services are designed to protect structures from water leakage, moisture damage, seepage, and dampness across roofs, terraces, basements, bathrooms, water tanks, and external walls. We use high-quality waterproofing materials and advanced application methods to ensure long-lasting protection and structural durability. Our skilled team carefully inspects every surface, identifies problem areas, and applies suitable waterproofing systems based on project requirements and environmental conditions. From new construction projects to repair and restoration works, we focus on delivering effective waterproofing solutions with proper execution, attention to detail, and dependable results that improve the life and strength of every structure.

Concrete Flooring

RS Constructions offers professional concrete flooring services for residential, commercial, industrial, and warehouse projects. We provide durable and smooth flooring solutions designed to withstand heavy loads, regular usage, and varying environmental conditions while maintaining long-term strength and performance. Our team handles every stage of the flooring process including surface preparation, leveling, reinforcement, concrete pouring, finishing, and curing using quality materials and modern construction techniques. We ensure accurate workmanship and proper finishing to achieve strong, even, and reliable concrete surfaces. From parking areas and industrial floors to residential spaces and commercial buildings, we focus on delivering high-quality concrete flooring solutions with timely execution, structural stability, and finishes that meet both functional and project-specific requirements.

Epoxy Flooring

RS Constructions provides high-quality epoxy flooring solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, and showroom spaces. Our epoxy flooring systems are designed to create smooth, durable, and attractive surfaces that resist stains, chemicals, moisture, abrasion, and heavy daily usage. We carry out complete flooring preparation including surface cleaning, crack filling, grinding, coating application, and finishing using premium epoxy materials and advanced installation methods. Our experienced team ensures proper bonding, seamless finishing, and long-lasting performance for every flooring project. From warehouses and factories to offices, parking areas, and retail spaces, we deliver epoxy flooring solutions that combine durability, easy maintenance, and modern appearance while meeting safety standards and project-specific flooring requirements.

Road Work

RS Constructions provides complete road work and infrastructure development services for residential layouts, commercial areas, industrial zones, and public projects. Our team handles all stages of road construction including site clearing, excavation, grading, sub-base preparation, compaction, drainage work, paving, and finishing. We use quality construction materials and modern equipment to ensure strong, smooth, and long-lasting roads capable of handling regular traffic and changing weather conditions. Our experienced engineers and skilled workforce follow proper construction standards to maintain safety, durability, and structural reliability throughout every project. From internal roads and parking areas to large roadway developments, we focus on efficient execution, proper planning, and timely completion while delivering durable road construction solutions that meet client requirements and industry standards.
