Services

Civil Construction

RS Constructions provides complete civil construction services for residential, commercial, and institutional projects. Our team handles all stages of construction including site preparation, excavation, compaction, leveling, foundation work, structural development, and finishing works. We focus on delivering strong and durable structures using high-quality materials and proven construction practices. Our experienced engineers and skilled workers ensure every stage of construction meets required safety standards and client specifications. From small building works to large construction projects, we ensure proper planning, efficient execution, and timely delivery while maintaining the highest level of workmanship and structural reliability.

Structural Work

We specialize in structural construction including frame structures, load-bearing structures, reinforced concrete structures, and steel reinforcement work. Our structural services are designed to create strong frameworks that support long-lasting and safe buildings. Our team carefully follows structural drawings and engineering standards to ensure stability and durability in every project. From column and beam structures to slab construction and reinforcement installation, every stage is handled with precision. By combining skilled workmanship with quality materials, RS Constructions ensures that each structural component contributes to the strength and longevity of the entire building.

Waterproofing

RS Constructions provides reliable waterproofing solutions designed to protect buildings from water leakage, dampness, and structural damage. Our waterproofing services are essential for increasing the durability and life span of any structure. We offer a wide range of waterproofing techniques including crystallization waterproofing, acrylic waterproofing, epoxy based waterproofing, APP membrane waterproofing, polyurea waterproofing, and injection grouting. These advanced solutions are applied to roofs, basements, terraces, bathrooms, and foundations to prevent water penetration and protect structures from long-term deterioration.

Fabrication Work

Our fabrication services include stainless steel and mild steel design and structural fabrication for construction and interior applications. We provide customized fabrication solutions based on project requirements. Our services include truss work, aluminum partitions, glass partitions, interior framework structures, and other metal fabrication works used in modern construction projects. Using high-quality materials and skilled craftsmanship, we ensure strong, durable, and visually appealing fabricated structures that integrate perfectly with the overall building design.

Road Construction

RS Constructions undertakes road infrastructure projects including bitumen roads, concrete roads, and pedestrian footpath construction. Our road development services follow systematic procedures to ensure long-lasting and safe road surfaces. The construction process includes excavation, leveling, compaction, base preparation, and surface finishing. Each stage is carefully executed using proper equipment and industry construction standards. Our goal is to deliver durable road infrastructure that provides smooth transportation and withstands long-term environmental and traffic conditions.

Landscaping

We provide professional landscaping services that enhance the appearance and usability of outdoor environments. Our landscaping solutions include garden development, park landscaping, greenery planning, and site beautification. By combining natural elements such as plants, lawns, pathways, and decorative features, we create well-structured outdoor spaces that complement the surrounding infrastructure. Our landscaping services are suitable for residential projects, commercial spaces, institutions, and public areas, helping create visually appealing and well-maintained environments.
